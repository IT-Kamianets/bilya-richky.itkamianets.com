Bilya Richky Hotel: river, heritage, and calm

February 10, 2026 · 5 min read Guide

Bilya Richky Hotel is a cozy, ethno-style riverside retreat in Kamianets-Podilskyi. Set on the banks of the Smotrych river, it blends wood-and-stone interiors with quiet green space and easy access to the Old Town.

Overview

Guests come here to slow down: scenic canyon views, landscaped paths, and the soft sound of the river below. The hotel is a short drive from the Old Town while feeling tucked into nature. A 15th-century tower nearby shapes the atmosphere and ourist storytelling on the property.

Experiences to plan

Riverside chany

Outdoor hot tubs with herbal infusions, best enjoyed at sunset.

Old Town walks

Historic streets, bridges, and fortress views within minutes.

Day trips

Ask the front desk about Bakota, Khotyn Fortress, and canyon viewpoints.
